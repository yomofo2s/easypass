How to Register and Download SmileVoice

Why SmileVoice?

I cannot mention the amount of people that contacted me about SmileVoice. Why SmileVoice?

What Technology is behind SmileVoice?

The Technology behind SmileVoice is based on Voice over IP, also call IP Telephony. Simply a method and group of technologies for the delivery of voice communications and multimedia sessions over Internet Protocol networks such as Internet.

Lifespan of this technology?

This Technology is one of the latest innovation from Smile Communications, making voice experience cheap and reliable. Come to think of it. This technology has come to stay as long as there is Wi-fi.Then there will always be SmileVoice

You can also make voice calls, video calls and conference calls, and send SMS messages to your friends and family when you’re connected to any other data network. SmileVoice is the lowest call rate in Nigeria today!

How to Download and SmileVoice

Step 1 - DOWNLOAD

For android users click SmileVoice on GooglePlay store.

For iPhone users click SmileVoice on Apple Store

Step 2 - ACTIVATION

After the download, you will see the image as shown below. Enter the number provided by your Smile Representative and click on "GET AN ACTIVATION CODE"

Step 3 - VERIFICATION

You will be redirected to another webpage, where you will enter your registered Smile Number or an email

(Note this email maybe different from your personal email, so ask your smile Representative for your registered email).

Then click on Submit

A code will be sent to the email or phone number that you provided, so copy/get the code and return back into your SmileVoice App code box.

Step 4 - LOGIN

Click Login, if successful, your app will show ready as shown below. Then you can start calling any network across Nigeria.
